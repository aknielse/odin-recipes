Banana Milk

Description:

A popular South Korean drink, banana milk has recently become popular in the United States, due to it's unique use in boba and milk tea drinks. This is a sweet drink, often mixed with coffee or tea, with a hint of banana flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Ripe Bananas
  • 1 Cup of granulated white sugar
  • 1 Tbs of lime or lemon juice
  • 1 Tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 Cup of milk (can sub oat or nut milk)

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Peel and halve the bananas. Cut into 1 inch slices and place in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Pour in 1 cup of sugar.
  4. Add 1 tbs of lime or lemon juice.
  5. Add 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  6. Mix until banana chunks are fully coated in mixture.
  7. Lay out mixture onto oven safe tray and then put into the oven for 10-15 minutes or until browned.
  8. Once finished, let cool for 5 minutes and then put into tupperware bowl.
  9. You've now made your banana jam. Mix this with 1 cup of milk or coffee in a blender. Now you have banana milk!
