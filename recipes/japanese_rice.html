Japanese Rice

Description:

Nothing hits the spot like a freshly cooked bowl of rice. Whether you enjoy it plain or with curry or an entree, it's definitely a necessity in any household. Follow this recepe to learn how to make it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 to 3 cups of Nishiki short-grain rice
  • 1.5 to 4.5 cups of water
  • Rice cooker or pressure cooker.

Steps:

  1. Measure out 1 to 3 cups of rice (depending on how many people are eating.
  2. Rinse rice 3-4 times until water is clear.
  3. Put the rinsed rice in pot and level-out.
  4. Place your hand on top of the rice and pour water in until it reaches just past your first knuckle. The water ratio should be about 1:1.5 rice to water.
  5. If using rice cooker:
    1. Place the rice in the rice cooker with lid and set on cook until finished.
    2. Use spoon or Japanese rice spatula to mix rice (use steam from lid to coat spatula. This makes it easier to mix as the rice won't stick as easily.
    3. Enjoy your rice!
  6. If using pressure cooker:
    1. Place the pot with rice in pressure cooker and cover with lid.
    2. Set the pressure cooker to 'high pressure' and cook for 8 minutes.
    3. Use manual release to release pressure.
    4. Use spoon or Japanese rice spatula to mix rice (use steam from lid to coat spatula. This makes it easier to mix as the rice won't stick as easily.
    5. Enjoy your rice!
